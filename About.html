Ananya Sharma

Ananya Sharma is the passionate founder of Sambhanvan, blending Indian tradition with modern innovation. After completing her MBA and working in corporate India, she started her business in 2016 with a vision to deliver quality and authenticity.

Ananya is committed to empowering women, promoting sustainability, and giving back to her community. Her journey is one of resilience, culture, and entrepreneurial spirit.
